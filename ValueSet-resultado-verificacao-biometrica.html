ValueSet: Resultado da Verificação Biométrica Facial

Official URL: https://biometria.gointerop.com/fhir/ValueSet/resultado-verificacao-biometrica Version: 0.1.0
Active as of 2026-03-20 Computable Name: ResultadoVerificacaoBiometrica

Indica o resultado da comparação biométrica entre a foto capturada e o template armazenado.

Logical Definition (CLD)

  • Include estes códigos, tal como definidos em http://terminology.hl7.org/CodeSystem/v3-ObservationInterpretation versão 📍4.0.0
    CódigoEcrãDefinição
    POSPositivo (verificado)A presence finding of the specified component / analyte, organism or clinical sign based on the established threshold of the performed test or procedure.
    NEGNegativo (não verificado)An absence finding of the specified component / analyte, organism or clinical sign based on the established threshold of the performed test or procedure.

    \[Note: Negative does not necessarily imply the complete absence of the specified item.\]
    INDIndeterminado (erro de sistema)The specified component / analyte, organism or clinical sign could neither be declared positive / negative nor detected / not detected by the performed test or procedure.

    *Usage Note:* For example, if the specimen was degraded, poorly processed, or was missing the required anatomic structures, then "indeterminate" (i.e. "cannot be determined") is the appropriate response, not "equivocal".
